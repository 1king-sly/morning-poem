GOOD MORNING


        Over is the night
        Cometh the sun
        Giving its sparkling light
        To the daughter and son

        This beautiful day
        Is really wanting
        May you make the hay
        While it’s shining

        Come off the bed
        The moment has come
        Only lay the dead
        The still and calm

        It’s another day
        So wake up and smile
        Successfully you may
        When you open the life file.

        By Byrone Kinsly
